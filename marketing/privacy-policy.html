Privacy Policy

Quant Academy — Last updated: May 31, 2026

Quant Academy is designed with privacy first. We store all your learning data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Quant Academy ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally

All learning progress and app data is stored exclusively on your device using local SQLite database. This includes:

  • Lesson completion progress
  • XP (experience points) earned
  • Quiz results and scores
  • Achievements unlocked
  • Custom strategies you create
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Financial information
  • Contacts or media from your device

3. How We Use Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is used solely to:

  • Track your learning progress across modules
  • Display your XP level and achievements
  • Save your custom trading strategies
  • Provide a personalized learning experience

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties. Since we do not collect data, there is nothing to share.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database with WAL (Write-Ahead Logging) mode for data integrity. The data is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, etc.).

6. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • In-App Deletion: You can delete all your data from within the app by going to Profile > Settings > Delete All Data. This permanently removes all progress, XP, achievements, strategies, and settings.
  • App Uninstallation: Uninstalling the app automatically removes all associated data from your device.

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your progress cannot be recovered.

7. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics
  • Facebook SDK or any social media SDKs
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services that collect personal data

8. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The educational content is designed for users interested in quantitative finance and is appropriate for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no risk of inadvertent collection of children's information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: All your data is already accessible to you on your device.
  • Right to Deletion: You can delete all data at any time through the app settings.
  • Right to Portability: Since data is stored locally, it is already in your possession.
  • Right to Object: We do not process data for marketing or profiling purposes.
